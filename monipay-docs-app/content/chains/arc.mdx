# Arc

Arc is a next-generation blockchain network supported by Monipay for gasless stablecoin payments via MoniTag.

## Supported tokens

| Token | Contract |
|-------|----------|
| USDC  | Coming soon |

## How to use Monipay on Arc

1. Open [monipay.xyz](https://monipay.xyz) and connect your Arc-compatible wallet.
2. Select **Arc** as your active network.
3. Send to any `@monitag` or social username - gas is sponsored.

## MoniBot on Arc

MoniBot commands work the same across all chains. To pay on Arc specifically, your wallet must be on the Arc network when you connect.

```
!monibot pay @username $10 USDC
```
