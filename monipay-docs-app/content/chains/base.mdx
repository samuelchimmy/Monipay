# Monipay on Base

Base is Monipay's primary home for gasless USDC payments.

## Network Details

- **Chain ID:** `8453`
- **Token:** USDC
- **Decimals:** 6
- **Relayer Model:** ERC-2771 Meta-Transactions
- **Builder Code:** `bc_qt9yxo1d` (ERC-8021)
- **Explorer:** [Basescan](https://basescan.org)

## Gas Sponsorship

Monipay sponsors 100% of the gas fees for P2P payments on Base. New accounts receive a small gas grant (0.000002 ETH) on signup to initialize the account if necessary, though the relayer handles the vast majority of user interactions.

## Platform Fee

A 1% platform fee is collected on every transaction, split atomically by the router contract.
