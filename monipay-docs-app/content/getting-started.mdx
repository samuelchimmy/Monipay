# Quick start

Monipay turns any social handle into a payment address. You don't need a wallet app, a seed phrase, or gas tokens.

Three steps to your first payment:

1. **[Create your MoniTag](/docs/getting-started/create-monitag)** | a 3–20 character handle that becomes your universal payment identity across Base, BSC, Solana, Tempo and Celo.
2. **[Fund your wallet](/docs/getting-started/fund-wallet)** | send USDC, USDT, or αUSD (AlphaUSD) from any exchange to the address Monipay generated for you.
3. **[Send your first payment](/docs/getting-started/send-payment)** | pay anyone by `@monitag` with zero gas.

## What you get

- A non-custodial wallet you fully own (see the [Walkaway Test](/docs/concepts/walkaway-test)).
- Gasless transfers on every supported chain.
- One identity that works in [Discord](/docs/monibot/discord), [Telegram](/docs/monibot/telegram), and [X](/docs/monibot/twitter) through MoniBot.
