# Fund your wallet

Monipay makes it easy to deposit stablecoins from any external source.

## How to Deposit

1. Tap the **Fund** button in the app.
2. Select the chain you wish to deposit to (Base, BSC, Solana, etc.).
3. Copy your deposit address or scan the QR code.
4. Send the supported stablecoin from your exchange (Binance, Coinbase) or external wallet (MetaMask, Phantom).

## Detection

Monipay polls the blockchain RPCs for incoming transactions. Once a deposit is detected on-chain (usually within 30 seconds), the app will show a success animation and update your balance.

## Supported Tokens

| Chain | Token |
|-------|-------|
| Base  | USDC  |
| BSC   | USDT  |
| Solana| USDC (SPL)|
| Tempo | AlphaUSD |
| Celo  | USDm  |
