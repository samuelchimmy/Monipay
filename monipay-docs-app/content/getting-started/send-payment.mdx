# Send a payment

Sending stablecoins on Monipay is as simple as sending a text message.

## Steps to Send

1. Tap **Send** on the home screen.
2. Enter the recipient's `@MoniTag`.
3. Enter the amount you wish to send.
4. Select the chain (if multiple balances are available).
5. Confirm the payment with your 6-digit PIN.

## Settlement

Payments are signed locally on your device and relayed to the blockchain. Settlement typically occurs within seconds. You can view the transaction hash and explorer link immediately after the payment is broadcast.

## Zero Gas

Monipay sponsors the gas for all P2P sends. A 1% platform fee is applied to the transaction to cover infrastructure costs.
