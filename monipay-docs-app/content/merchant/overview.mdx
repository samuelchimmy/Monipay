# Monipay for merchants

Monipay provides a complete suite of tools for businesses to accept stablecoins.

## Key Offerings

- **Hosted Storefront:** Sell products at `monipay.xyz/store/@yourtag`.
- **Invoicing:** Create and track professional on-chain invoices.
- **Payment Gateway:** Embed Monipay into your existing checkout flow.
- **API & Webhooks:** Integrate payment data with your backend.

## Pricing

- **Personal:** Free (gas sponsored).
- **Merchant Transacitons:** 1% per transaction.
- **Storefront Pro:** $30/month for advanced customization.
