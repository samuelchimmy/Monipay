# Merchant storefront

Turn your MoniTag into a full-featured online store.

## Features

- **Personalized URL:** `monipay.xyz/store/@yourtag`.
- **Product Management:** Add titles, descriptions, images, and prices.
- **Cart System:** Supports up to 99 items per order.
- **Shipping Forms:** Optional collection of customer delivery info.
- **Success Redirect:** Automated 2.5s redirect back to your specified URL.
