# MoniBot for Telegram

MoniBot on Telegram mirrors the Discord implementation with platform-specific adaptations for the Telegram Bot API.

## Commands

- `!send @username $5` , Send stablecoins to another Telegram user.
- `/link @monitag` , Link your Telegram handle to your MoniTag.
- `/balance` , Check your Monipay balances.

## Linking Social Identity

MoniBot uses `return_to` callbacks to securely link your Telegram account to your Monipay identity. Once linked, you can receive payments sent to your Telegram handle directly into your Monipay wallet.
