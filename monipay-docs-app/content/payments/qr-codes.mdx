# QR code payments

Accept payments in person using Monipay's branded QR codes.

## Branded QRs

Monipay generates high-resolution, branded QR codes for every user. These QRs are "pixel-locked" to ensure reliability across all scanning devices.

## Scan-to-Pay

1. Customer scans the QR code using their phone camera or the Monipay app.
2. The browser/app opens a pre-filled payment screen.
3. Customer confirms the amount and signs with their PIN.
4. Merchant receives a real-time notification of the successful payment.
