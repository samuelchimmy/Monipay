# MoniPay Documentation

**MoniPay** is a gasless, non-custodial, multi-chain stablecoin payment network. Send USDC, USDT, AlphaUSD or USDC SPL by **MoniTag™ username** instead of long hex addresses, with **zero gas fees** for the user. MoniPay is a Hammer, not a service — your keys live on your device, encrypted with your PIN, and the platform passes the **Walkaway Test**: if MoniPay disappears tomorrow, your funds are still spendable from any compatible wallet.

This documentation is the canonical reference for MoniPay's product, MoniTag identity layer, MoniBot AI agent, merchant gateway, mobile apps, smart contracts, and integration APIs.
