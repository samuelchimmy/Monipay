# MoniTag™

A **MoniTag™** is your permanent username on MoniPay. It resolves to your wallet on every supported chain — Base, BSC, Solana, Tempo and beyond. One identity, every network.

> **MoniTag™** is always written with a **lowercase `m`**, an **uppercase `T`**, and the **™** trademark symbol. Examples: `@samuel`, `@coffeeshop`, `@dao_treasury`.

## Why MoniTag instead of an address?

Crypto addresses are 42 hexadecimal characters with no error correction. One typo and your money is gone forever. A MoniTag is:

- **Memorable** — `@samuel` vs `0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f0bEb1`
- **Cross-chain** — same MoniTag resolves on Base, BSC, Solana, Tempo
- **Human-validated** — you can read it back to verify
- **Permanent** — once claimed, it's yours

## Naming rules

- 3–20 characters
- Lowercase letters (`a–z`), digits (`0–9`), dot (`.`), underscore (`_`)
- Cannot start or end with `.` or `_`
- Cannot be on the [reserved list](/monitag/reserved-usernames) (~150 brand and generic terms)

## Linking social accounts

Optionally connect Twitter/X, Discord and Telegram to your MoniTag. This unlocks **MoniBot** social payments — receive money replied to your tweet or DM'd to you on Discord. Linking is via OAuth handshake (Discord) or `return_to` callbacks (Telegram). You can unlink at any time from Settings.
