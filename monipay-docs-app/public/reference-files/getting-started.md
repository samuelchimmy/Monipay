# Glossary

**AlphaUSD (αUSD)** — Tempo's primary stablecoin. 18 decimals. TIP-20 standard.

**Hammer** — MoniPay's product philosophy. A tool you own and walk away with, not a service you depend on.

**MoniBot** — MoniPay's autonomous AI agent for social payments on Twitter, Discord, Telegram.

**MoniPay** — The product. A gasless, non-custodial, multi-chain stablecoin wallet.

**MoniTag™** — Your permanent username on MoniPay. Lowercase `m`, uppercase `T`, ™.

**Paymaster** — A relayer that pays gas on the user's behalf in exchange for the user's signature on an EIP-712 typed-data message.

**TIP-20** — Tempo's stablecoin standard. ERC-20-like, with on-chain transfer memos.

**Walkaway Test** — The principle that if MoniPay disappears, your funds remain spendable from any compatible wallet.
