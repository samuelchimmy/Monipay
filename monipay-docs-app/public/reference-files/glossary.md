# Reserved MoniTag usernames

To protect users and brands, MoniPay reserves ~150 terms from the MoniTag namespace. Reserved categories:

- **MoniPay-internal:** `monipay`, `monibot`, `monitag`, `support`, `admin`, `team`, `official`, `staff`
- **Generic financial:** `bank`, `wallet`, `treasury`, `bitcoin`, `ethereum`, `usdc`, `usdt`
- **Major brands:** `coinbase`, `binance`, `metamask`, `phantom`, `circle`, `tether`, `base`, `solana`, `tempo`
- **Offensive content** and common scam impersonation targets
- **Single characters and very short tags** (≤2 chars)

Attempting to register a reserved tag returns the exact error:

> "That MoniTag is reserved. Please choose another."

If you have a legitimate trademark claim to a reserved tag, email **support@monipay.xyz** with proof of trademark ownership.
