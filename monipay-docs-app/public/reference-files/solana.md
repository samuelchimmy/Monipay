# Brand & MoniTag™ usage

## MoniTag™ casing

Always **lowercase `m`**, **uppercase `T`**, with the **™** symbol on first use:

- ✓ MoniTag™
- ✓ Your MoniTag is `@samuel`
- ✗ monitag, Monitag, MONITAG

## Em-dash policy

Do not use em dashes (—) on chain-specific marketing pages (`/base`, `/bsc`, `/solana`, `/tempo`). Use periods or commas. This is a deliberate copy constraint.

## Banned phrases

Never write **"Not Just"** in MoniPay copy. It's a tired construction and our brand voice avoids it.

## Aesthetic

- **Trantor minimal** — F5F5F7 background light / 0E0F14 background dark
- **DM Sans** typography
- Primary dark-mode CTAs are **light** (inverted)
- **Locked black 'Moni'** on receipt strips, regardless of theme
- Hollow logo dot for a 0-balance dashboard, filled blue dot when funds are present
